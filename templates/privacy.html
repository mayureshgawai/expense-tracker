Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Spendly collects, how it's used, and how it's protected.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly, such as your account details (name, email, password) and the expense records you log, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide core Spendly features — storing your expenses, generating spending summaries, and helping you understand your financial habits. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data is stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers solely to operate and host the service.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
